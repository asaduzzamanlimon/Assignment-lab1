President of BCB

NAZMUL HASSAN, MP

Nazmul Hasan Papon is the president of Bangladesh Cricket Board. Papon was born on May 31, 1961. He has two sisters Tania Rahman and Oanima Bakht. His father was the president of Bangladesh Zillur Rahman. He completed his MBA from Institute of Business Administration, University of Dhaka. He was elected unanimously He is the first elected president of the board with his predecessors being appointed by the Bangladesh Government. He is a member of parliament from Bangladesh Awami League.
